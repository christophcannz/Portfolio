Hello, My name is Christopher Cannady. I am a web developer and graphic designer based
out of Minneapolis, Minnesota. My lifelong passion for design and layout has led me to constantly
find ways to allow my creativity to come to life in a professional manner. It all started in grade school
where my sheets of homework would oftentimes be overcome by sketches of basketball logos.
This may have made grading difficult for my teachers, however it was what led me to get my
Bachelor of Science Degree in Graphic Design from The Art Institutes International Minnesota.
From there, I begin to toy around with web design using Yahoo! GeoCities and Adobe Dreamweaver
which eventually led me to seek a deeper knowledge in the subject matter. Most recently, I received my
Full-Stack Web Developer Certificate from the University of Minnesota College of Continuing & Professional Studies.
Please take a moment to view my portfolio content and feel free to contact me. Thank you.
