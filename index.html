I am a web developer and graphic designer based out of Minneapolis, Minnesota. My lifelong enthusiasm
for design and layout has led me to constantly find ways to allow my creativity to come to life in a
professional manner. It all started in grade school where my sheets of homework would oftentimes be
overcome by sketches of basketball logos. This may have made grading difficult for my teachers, however
it was what led me to get my Bachelor of Science Degree in Graphic Design from the Art Institutes International
Minnesota. From there, I begin to toy around with web design using Yahoo! GeoCities which eventually led me
to seek a deeper knowledge of how websites are engineered. Most recently, I received my Full Stack Web Developer
Certificate from the University of Minnesota College of Continuing & Professional Studies. Thank you for Visiting!
